# NSU Academic Requirements Knowledge Base

## 1. Waiver Course Rules

These courses are foundational. If waived, they do not count toward the total credit requirement.

- **ENG102** (Introduction to Composition): 3 Credits. Waived based on SAT/IELTS scores or high admission test performance.
- **BUS112** (Elementary Mathematics): 3 Credits. Waived for BBA students via SAT (1150+) or high admission test scores.
- **MAT112** (College Algebra): 3 Credits. Waived for Economics students based on entry test.
- **MAT116** (Pre-Calculus): 3 Credits. Waived for CSE/Engineering students based on entry test.

## 4. Financial Aid & Performance

### Admission Merit
100% tuition waiver for top 10 scorers in the admission test.

### Academic Standing
Students must maintain a **2.75 CGPA** and carry at least **9 credits per semester** to be eligible for ongoing financial aid.

### Sibling Waiver
25% waiver for each sibling if both are currently enrolled.

## 5. Grading Scale (NSU Standard)

|-----------------|--------------|--------------|---------------|--------------|
| 93 and above   | A (Excellent) | 4.0          | ✅ Yes        | ✅ Yes       |

| 70 - 72        | C-            | 1.7          | ✅ Yes        | ✅ Yes       |

**Minimum passing grade for credit:** **D** (60-66, 1.0 grade points)

**Key Rules:**
- F grades earn **no credit** but are included in GPA calculation as 0.0
- I and W grades earn **no credit** and are **excluded from GPA calculation**
- Only courses required for degree are included in GPA calculation
- +/- modifiers adjust grade points by 0.3

## 6. Retake Policy

### Course Repetition Rules
- Students may repeat courses with grade **'B' or lower**
- When retaking: **Only the best grade** is used to calculate CGPA
- **F grade handling:** 
  - F grade stays in CGPA calculation until course is retaken
  - Once retaken, only the better grade counts
  - Applies to all NSU students regardless of enrollment date
- Student must register and pay tuition for retaken courses

---

## 7. Academic Standing Rules

- **Good Standing:** CGPA ≥ 2.0
- **Probation:** CGPA < 2.0
- **Dismissal:** CGPA < 2.0 for two consecutive semesters

---

## 8. Incomplete (I) Grade Policy

- Given only at end of semester for work in progress
- Must file Incomplete Grade form with Registrar
- Work must be completed within **one semester**
- If not completed, reverts to tentative grade (or F if no tentative assigned)

---

## 9. Withdrawal (W) Grade Policy

- Assigned when student officially drops course between weeks 3-12
- Prior to week 3: No entry on transcript
- **W does not affect GPA**
